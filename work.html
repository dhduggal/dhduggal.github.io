My Work Experience

DMI Finance - Summer 2019

During the summer of 2019, I worked at DMI Finance in New Delhi. Here I created credit risk models to predict the probability of default for 'New to Credit' customers for partners ZestMoney and Samsung. These models were created using Python ,Scikit-Learn, SQL and Athena(Amazon Serverless Interactive Query Service). During my time at DMI, I was responsible for extracting and cleaning data, Visualizing and treating data for outliers and null values, dealing with an imbalanced dataset (using SMOTE oversampling), creating a gradient boost and logistic regression model in order to to minimize decile based risk. During my time here I learnt a great deal about Machine Learning, Fin-Tech Companies and common API's of relational database.

Alchemy X- Spring & Summer 2018

Alchemy was an on-campus startup (in San Diego, California) that I worked at alongside classes during spring and summer quarters in 2018. While working at Alchemy I created Python scripts to scrape data from Instagram and crawl through different users and hashtags using modules Requests & BS4. From the data that was collected, I created a graph of users/hashtags that are similar with the end goal of using the graph to provide customers with access to pages with similar content and smaller following for advertising purposes (mainly to advertise to a smaller group of people at lower costs). As a side project during my time here I also created an automated DM'ing service, that broadcasted messages to large groups of users on Instagram.

Accenture - Summer 2017

In summer quarter of first year I worked in the data analysis and customer care department at Accenture for client, Airtel at their network operations center in Haryana. During this internship I was responsible for compiling data from call logs to check how many complaints from corporate clients were being resolved within 24 hours of their logging. I helped in visualizing this data and presenting it to upper management.
